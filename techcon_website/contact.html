Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find answers to common questions in our FAQ section. Here are some quick answers to popular inquiries:

Registration & Tickets

  • Q: Can I transfer my registration to someone else?
    A: Yes, you can transfer your registration up to 30 days before the event by contacting our registration team.
  • Q: Is there a refund policy?
    A: Full refunds are available up to 60 days before the event. After that, a 50% refund is available up to 30 days before.

Event Information

  • Q: What should I bring to the conference?
    A: Bring your confirmation email, business cards, a laptop/tablet for workshops, and comfortable walking shoes.
  • Q: Is WiFi available at the venue?
    A: Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the venue.

Office Hours & Response Times

Email Response Time: We aim to respond to all emails within 24 hours during business days.

Phone Support: Available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM PST. For urgent matters outside business hours, please email us and mark your message as urgent.

Live Chat: Available on our website during business hours for immediate assistance with registration and general questions.
